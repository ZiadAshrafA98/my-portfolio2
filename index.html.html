My journey.

I'm Ziad Ashraf, a Product Designer based in Cairo, Egypt.

I've spent 3+ years designing end-to-end across mobile apps, web platforms, and games — shipping products used by 500K+ people and launching apps that hit 1,300+ sign-ups in two weeks.

I specialise in 0-to-1 product design, building design systems from scratch, and closing the gap between design and development through interaction design, motion, and illustration. I've worked across GCC, European, and global markets at Blink22 and Path2Live.

Outside client work, I'm drawn to products that make everyday life feel more considered — which is how Noor came to exist: a concept for a smart desk display that stays out of your way until you actually need it.

Good design is intentional. I start every project by understanding the problem before touching a single frame.
